Welcome! In the following you have the possibility to answer a quiz that deals with our notion of authenticity in times of virulent automatic machine learning. In recent years, advancements in technology have made it increasingly difficult for individuals to distinguish reality from fiction. The advent of deepfakes, bots, and artificial intelligence (AI) have allowed for the manipulation of images, videos, and audio recordings making it challenging to determine the authenticity of media.

Additionally, the use of bots on social media platforms can spread false information at a rapid pace, creating confusion and mistrust among users. AI algorithms can also be used to personalize news feeds and search results, potentially creating echo chambers where individuals are only exposed to information that confirms their existing beliefs.

To combat these challenges, it is important for individuals to be critical when consuming media and to fact-check information from multiple sources. In order to train our critical approach towards machine-generated content you can play the following Quiz-Game.
